# EcoPulse

EcoPulse is a carbon intelligence platform that helps people understand, track, and reduce their environmental impact — powered by AI, real-world emission data, and behavioral science.

Live at **[bhalla.info/projects/ecopulse](https://bhalla.info/projects/ecopulse)**

---

## What it does

EcoPulse gives users a clear picture of their carbon footprint across transport, food, energy, and shopping — and actively helps them reduce it.

- **Activity logging** with live emission estimates from Climatiq's 1M+ factor database
- **AI sustainability coach** powered by Google Gemini, with responses grounded in the user's actual carbon data
- **Receipt scanner** that uses Google Cloud Vision OCR to estimate the carbon cost of purchases
- **Carbon Simulator** — a personal "what if" tool that models the impact of switching to public transit, going vegetarian, working from home, and more
- **Weekly AI reports** summarizing progress and surfacing personalized reduction opportunities
- **Challenges and streaks** (Duolingo-style) to build consistent low-carbon habits
- **Global leaderboard** with real-time rankings
- **Live electricity grid intensity** from Electricity Maps, so users know when their grid is cleanest
